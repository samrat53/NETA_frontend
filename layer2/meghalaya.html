MEGHALAYA

Scenic northeastern state with abundant rainfall. Known as the "Abode of Clouds." Breathtaking natural beauty with waterfalls and living root bridges. Hill stations like Shillong. Local cuisine includes Jadoh and Nakham Bitchi. Rich tribal culture with festivals like Wangala.

Cherrapunji

Known as one of the wettest places on Earth, Cherrapunji offers stunning landscapes with lush green valleys, cascading waterfalls, and living root bridges. The Nohkalikai Falls, Mawsmai Cave, and the Double Decker Living Root Bridge are some of its famous attractions.

Shillong

The capital city of Meghalaya, Shillong is renowned for its picturesque landscapes, pleasant weather, and colonial charm. Visitors can explore attractions like the Ward's Lake, Shillong Peak, Elephant Falls, and enjoy the vibrant music scene and local markets.
