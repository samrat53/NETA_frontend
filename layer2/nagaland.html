NAGALAND

Northeastern state known for natural beauty, hills, waterfalls. Influenced by Bengali and Tribal flavors in food. Located near Bangladesh. Humid subtropical climate. Rich cultural heritage with festivals like Kharchi Puja.

Kohima

The capital city of Nagaland, Kohima offers scenic beauty with its rolling hills and panoramic views. It is known for its rich history, war memorials like the Kohima War Cemetery, and the Naga Heritage Village, which showcases the culture and traditions of the Naga tribes.

Dzükou Valley

Located on the border of Nagaland and Manipur, Dzükou Valley is a picturesque destination known for its enchanting beauty. It is famous for its lush green meadows, vibrant flowers, and pristine streams. The valley is a popular trekking spot and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains.
Locate on GMap

Khonoma Village

Khonoma Village is a model green village and a cultural hotspot in Nagaland. It is known for its community-based conservation efforts and sustainable practices. The village offers scenic beauty with terraced fields, traditional houses, and a peaceful environment. Visitors can learn about the rich Naga culture, explore nature trails, and enjoy traditional cuisine.
