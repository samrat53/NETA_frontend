ARUNACHAL PRADESH

Serene northeastern state known for its pristine landscapes and Himalayan beauty. Varied climate with snow-capped mountains, lush valleys, and gushing rivers. Offers adventure opportunities like trekking, river rafting, and wildlife exploration in places like Tawang and Namdapha National Park.

Tawang

Located at an elevation of 10,000 feet, Tawang is a picturesque town nestled amidst the Himalayas. It is known for its stunning landscapes, including snow-capped mountains, serene lakes like the Pangong Tso, and the famous Tawang Monastery, the largest Buddhist monastery in India.

Ziro Valley

Ziro Valley is a beautiful and tranquil destination known for its lush green landscapes, rice fields, and picturesque villages. It offers breathtaking views, trekking opportunities, and a chance to experience the unique culture of the Apatani tribe. The Ziro Music Festival, held annually, is also a major attraction.

Namdapha National Park

Situated in the easternmost part of Arunachal Pradesh, Namdapha National Park is a haven for wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers. It is one of the largest protected areas in the Eastern Himalayas and is known for its rich biodiversity, including various species of mammals, birds, and butterflies. Trekking through the park allows visitors to experience its pristine beauty.
