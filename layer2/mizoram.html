Mizoram

Scenic northeastern state. Located near Myanmar and Bangladesh. Pleasant climate. Abundance of hills, valleys, and rivers. Unique Mizo cuisine with bamboo shoots and local herbs. Rich cultural heritage with vibrant festivals.

Aizawl

The capital city of Mizoram, known for its scenic beauty, vibrant culture, and panoramic views of the surrounding hills. It offers attractions like the Durtlang Hills, Solomon's Temple, and the bustling local markets.

Tlawng River

A picturesque river flowing through the heart of Mizoram, offering breathtaking views and opportunities for boating and picnic spots along its banks. The river is surrounded by lush greenery and is a tranquil retreat for nature lovers.

Vantawng Falls

One of Mizoram's most magnificent waterfalls, plunging from a height of over 750 feet. Surrounded by lush forests, Vantawng Falls is a mesmerizing sight and a popular destination for nature enthusiasts and photographers.
