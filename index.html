Tripura

Northeastern state known for natural beauty, hills, waterfalls. Influenced by Bengali and Tribal flavors in food. Located near Bangladesh. Humid subtropical climate. Rich cultural heritage with festivals like Kharchi Puja.

Mizoram

Scenic northeastern state. Located near Myanmar and Bangladesh. Pleasant climate. Abundance of hills, valleys, and rivers. Unique Mizo cuisine with bamboo shoots and local herbs. Rich cultural heritage with vibrant festivals.

Meghalaya

Scenic northeastern state with abundant rainfall. Known as the "Abode of Clouds." Breathtaking natural beauty with waterfalls and living root bridges. Hill stations like Shillong. Local cuisine includes Jadoh and Nakham Bitchi. Rich tribal culture with festivals like Wangala.

Manipur

Northeastern state with scenic landscapes and rich culture. Located near Myanmar. Pleasant climate. Famous lakes and hills. Unique Manipuri cuisine. Renowned for Manipuri dance and vibrant festivals like Lai Haraoba. Known as the "Land of Jewels."

Nagaland

Northeastern state known for its scenic beauty and vibrant tribal culture. Located near Myanmar. Pleasant climate. Rich biodiversity with lush hills and valleys. Naga cuisine includes smoked meat and bamboo shoot delicacies. Colorful festivals like Hornbill Festival showcase traditional dances.

Assam

Picturesque northeastern state with tea gardens, rich wildlife, and vibrant culture. Home to the Brahmaputra River and renowned for its Assam tea, and mesmerizing bihu festival and pitha. Diverse and enchanting, Assam offers a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural heritage.

Arunachal Pradesh

Serene northeastern state known for its pristine landscapes and Himalayan beauty. Varied climate with snow-capped mountains, lush valleys, and gushing rivers. Offers adventure opportunities like trekking, river rafting, and wildlife exploration.
